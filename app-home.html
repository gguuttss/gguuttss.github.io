Stabilis App

Welcome!

You have found your way to the unofficial Stabilis App interface as provided to you by Stabilis Labs. The Stabilis protocol enables existence of the stable asset STAB.

This interface consists of multiple pages:

The Swap page is home to a STAB/XRD pool, which can be utilized or provided liquidity to.

The Borrow page is where the magic happens: STAB is created by borrowing it against a collateral. Demanding overcollateralization ensures STAB is always fully backed by other assets.

The Manage Loans page allows the managing of taken out loans.

The Liquidations page is used to pay off loans that are in danger of being undercollateralized. For this, the liquidator is rewarded.

To prevent being unpleasantly surprised, it comes highly recommended to read the documentation before interacting with the interface!
